Després 60

19-20 June, 2025

With his broad range of mathematical interests, Bruno Després has made fundamental contributions to domain decomposition methods, the numerics of hyperbolic conservation laws, plasma physics, and, more recently, the foundations of scientific machine learning.

The aim of this workshop is to gather the community of colleagues and collaborators who, together with him, have been shaping these fields of research over the past decades.
